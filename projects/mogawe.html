Project Information

  • Category : Android & iOS (Flutter)

MoGawe is a B2B service product launched by PT. Media Baru Internasional that connects agencies or communities in need of freelance workers (crowdsourcing) and daily wage job seekers. MoGawe has several products, mobile App that used by crowdsourcing workers to get daily wage jobs and Business web App to manage the distribution of work released by business clients.
